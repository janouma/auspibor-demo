Genesis

This ancient book is made of a series of tales and legends.

To the astonishment of Archeologues, it is said that the texts are written in Anglo Normand language.

The grimoire gathers several stories which will be released along with the collections and upon their restoration.

In order to allow @AUSPIBOR to restore this book in a good schedule and with the best result, you can support us by purchasing models of the collection.

The current collection ''Spi line'' is deeply inspired from Book First chapter one.

The further @AUSPIBOR collections will be inspired accordingly by the other chapters.
