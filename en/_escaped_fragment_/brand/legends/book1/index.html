Ancient Book

This ancient book has been discovered by a member of the future AUSPIBOR Team in the wreck of a boat found at the bottom of the ocean.

In spite of the case protecting those writtings, this ancient book was in very bad shape. Nevertheless enough well preserved, the team opted to start restoring.

At the moment we are restoring the first tale.

The name AUSPIBOR has been inspired by this first legend telling the story of the encounter between Austral and Boreal.

To illustrate this first chapter, a SPI SHIRT line has been developed, inspired by Austral.

The book restauration being laborious, it will be impossible to release the full content in one shot.

It is the reason why, as far as possible, we are commited to offering for each tale restored a new AUSPIBOR collection.
